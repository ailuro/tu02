Theme Info

This theme’s style is very simple, and everything you need should be found by hovering over the icon, description and post! Asker names are shown by hovering over the asker portrait.

The default colours are as shown on this demo blog, and you are perfectly welcome to leave them as they are if you really want to.

Any questions should be directed to my theme blog. I will try my best to fix any errors I can!
